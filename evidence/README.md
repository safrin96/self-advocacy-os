# Evidence Folder

Drop your supporting files here. Claude Code will read everything in this folder when building your prep packet.

## What to include

- **Project notes** - descriptions of projects you led or contributed to, with outcomes
- **Brag log** - a running list of accomplishments (start one if you do not have one)
- **Feedback** - quotes from managers, peers, clients, stakeholders (note who said it and when)
- **Metrics** - numbers that show your impact: time saved, revenue influenced, users served, errors reduced
- **Scope documentation** - anything that shows your responsibilities expanded beyond your job description
- **Role expectations** - your original job description, if you have it (useful for showing the gap between title and actual work)

## Format

Plain text (.md or .txt) works best. You can also use .doc or .pdf files.

Name your files descriptively:
- `project-notes.md`
- `feedback-q1-2026.md`
- `metrics-dashboard-impact.md`

## Sanitizing

Remove confidential information before adding files here. See `docs/privacy.md` for guidance.

Replace names, client identifiers, and proprietary dollar amounts. Keep the structure and ratios.
