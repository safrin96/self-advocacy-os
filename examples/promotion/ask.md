# The Ask

## Primary ask

Promotion from PM II to Senior PM, effective the October cycle.

## Fallback ask

If the promotion is not approved for October:
1. A written development plan with specific criteria I need to meet
2. A formal 6-month check-in with my manager and the PMO Director
3. Assignment to one cross-org program as the named program lead (not "supporting")

## What I will not accept

"Keep doing what you are doing and we will see." Without specific criteria, there is no way to know what is enough.
