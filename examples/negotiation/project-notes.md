# Project Notes

## Analytics Migration (Jan-Mar)
Migrated 12 legacy reports from manual Excel processes to automated Power BI dashboards. Reduced average report generation time from 4 hours to 45 minutes per cycle. Marketing and Operations teams now self-serve their weekly metrics instead of requesting ad-hoc pulls.

## Client Account Coverage (Feb-Mar)
Manager traveled internationally for 6 weeks. I was the primary point of contact for 3 client accounts:
- Client A: Monthly analytics review. Delivered on time for Feb and March.
- Client B: Escalation about data discrepancy. Investigated, found the source (a join condition in the ETL), fixed it, communicated resolution to the client within 24 hours.
- Client C: Requested a new quarterly report. Scoped it, built it, delivered it. Client approved on first review.

No escalations during the entire 6-week period.

## Dashboard Build (Feb)
The sales team was tracking pipeline in a shared Google Sheet. It broke every Monday because multiple people edited it simultaneously. I built a Power BI dashboard connected to the CRM. The sales lead now uses it in every Monday pipeline meeting.

## VP Board Summary (April)
The VP of Operations needed a summary of the data migration impact for her board presentation. I wrote a one-page brief: what changed, what improved, what's next. She used it directly. My name was not on the presentation.
