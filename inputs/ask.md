# The Ask

## Primary ask

What do you want? Be specific.
(Example: "A title change to Senior Analyst and a compensation adjustment reflecting the expanded scope I have carried for 6 months.")

## Fallback ask

If the primary is declined, what would still be useful?
(Example: "A written review date, documented senior-level responsibilities, and a professional development budget.")

## What you will not accept

What outcome would you walk away from or push back on?
(Example: "Being told 'we'll revisit next year' with no written timeline.")
